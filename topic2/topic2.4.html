Discrimination

Sex Discrimination


occurs when someone is treated less favourably, or not given the same opportunities as a person of a different sex, because of their sex. It also occurs when a rule or policy applies to everyone but disadvantages a person based on his or her sex and the policy is not reasonable.

Sexual Orientation


Sexual orientation discrimination happens when a person is treated less favourably than another person in a similar situation because he or she has a sexual orientation towards persons of the same sex; persons of a different sex; or persons of the same sex and persons of a different sex.
