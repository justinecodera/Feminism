Equality and Discrimination

How is equality and discrimination related to feminism on what terms?


Equality and discrimination is really a big topic that is still happening today. Feminism's aim to ensure the equal rights as well as the opportunities to all women. While the equality, all the genders aim is to have a treatment without any discrimination. And the discrimination is an unfair treatment to all people and groups based on their characteristics, colour, height, skin, gender, and so on.

Equality and discrimination is related to feminism because all of us are wanting to have equality in our lives. Most of the time, women are being judged by others because of their gender and also in the reason of, we grow up on the place that told us women are only for the family or in homes, while men are the one who is strong that needs to work for the family. For example, our ancestors' history is that all of the women are not allowed to go to school nor to work the males job. In that reason, feminism is related to discrimination that thinks women are weak and cannot live for their own. It is also related to equality because of the assumptions that men and women are different, by that feminism is based on women having a freedom.

In conclusion, equality feminist advocates for the equal rights and standing of both men and women regardless of their religion, colour, height, gender, desires, wants, and so on. Aside from that, feminism can be seen as a movement to end the sexism, sexist exploitation, and to successfuly achieve full the gender identity.
